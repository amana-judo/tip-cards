TIP 03

What are the Platform Pillars of AEP?

01
Bring Your Own Agents
Port doesn't lock you into a specific agent. Bring Claude, Cursor, custom-built agents — anything. Port is the single pane of glass that sits on top of all of them.
02
Context Lake
All agent knowledge in one place — services, owners, dependencies, CI/CD metadata, runbooks, policies. Always up to date. Higher accuracy, fewer tokens.
03
Agent Management
Auto-discover and catalog every agent across your org. See owner, model, version, production-readiness, and permitted tools — a service catalog, but for agents.
04
Skills Registry
Manage, certify, and distribute reusable agent capabilities from one place. Teams stop reinventing the wheel. Standardization, open-source skill sharing, and governance built in.
05
MCP Hub
Full visibility into every MCP connector in your org — which are active, approved, and which agents consume them. You can't govern what you can't see.
06
Human in the Loop
Define exactly when humans approve agent actions, get visibility into decision-making, and set the boundary between autonomous and supervised — based on risk.
07
Orchestration
The connective tissue between humans, agents, and tools. Build end-to-end agentic workflows — self-healing incidents, autonomous ticket resolution, security remediation.
08
Governance
Define what actions agents can take, when, and under what constraints. Set cost limits per agent or team, enforce policies, and maintain a full audit log. No black boxes.
09
AI ROI
Measure the actual impact of your agentic workflows — time saved, money saved, risk eliminated. Track agent quality over time so you can continuously improve and justify the investment.
